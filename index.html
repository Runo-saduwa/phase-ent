About Us

Phase Enterprises is a duly registered Nigerian Company. Our main business focus is Agricultural productions and/or manufacturing with special strength in manufacturing of Straw building materials especially Doors and Balustrades.

Specifically, we are into Animal husbandry, hydroponics farming of Tomatoes & Capsicums (fresh pepper), Export/sales of Agricultural crops like Cocoa, Coffee, Ginger, Dry chilli pepper etc. Our farm & factory is situated at km 22, along Ondo/Ore Express road, Bagbe town, Ondo Sate. We have a wide networks of contacts in states like Taraba, Plateau, Adamawa, Oyo, Osun, Ondo, Ogun, Lagos, Edo, Kwara, Kogi, Niger, Kaduna, Benue, Cross River, Akwa Ibom and some neighbouring west African countries.

Our strength lies in the direct productions and/or sourcing of our high quality products at affordable prices within the reach of everybody.
